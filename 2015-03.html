DCON

When?

March 27th to March 29th

Where?

Carpool:

8:15 am

Details

CNH, it's time to put your magnifying glass in your backpack and get your detective hats on because District Convention is coming to the Woodland Hills Marriott Hotel from March 27th to 29th 2015 and it is no MYSTERY why you don’t want to miss out on this once in a lifetime event. District Convention 2015 will put to use your detective know-hows as you navigate your way around the elections of our new District Board officers, the wide array of educational and fun workshops for new and returning members, an Awards Night that celebrates all of our District's amazing accomplishments, a jam-packed Saturday night of talent, dancing, and everything under the SUNNY sun of CNH. It’ll take a true sleuth to really figure out how many unforgettable memories you’ll make this weekend! So CNH, it’s time to put your detective skills to the test. Help us find Sunny Jr. before DCON 2015 and come out for a weekend that will leave you inspired and fired up about everything Circle K. Your search for service, leadership, and fellowship ends here at District Convention 2015.

Aldrin Santamaria: president@nevadacki.com
