District Summer Service Social

When?

August 15th to 16th

Where?

San Diego, CA

Carpool:

August 15th, 6:00 am @ Joe Shuttle Stop

Details

This year's DSSS is from August 15th-16th at the Town and Country Resort in San Diego, CA and it's all about "Cooking up Service" with a variety of service events that include creating backpacks to donate to underprivileged children, making "Thank You" cards for the San Diego Fire Department, and making sack lunches for the homeless of the San Diego area. Also built into the itinerary of DSSS are unique socials such as laser tagging and fresh workshops designed to propel your Circle K to starting the new school year with a "bang"! After DSSS ends on the 16th, we may stay another day in/around San Diego for a SoCal club social!
