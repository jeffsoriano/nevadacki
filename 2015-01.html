American Heart Association Fantasy Wedding Faire

When?

January 25th, 9:00 am to 12:00 pm and 12:00 pm to 3:30 pm

Where?

Carpool:

8:30 am & 11:30 am @ Joe Shuttle Stop

Details

In its 28th year, the American Heart Association's Fantasy Wedding Faire is the longest running and one of the largest shows in Nevada!
The mission of the American Heart Association is to build healthier lives, free of cardiovascular diseases and stroke. By participating in or attending this event you are helping us expand our reach and contributing to our live saving research.
