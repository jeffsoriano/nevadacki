Fall Training Conference

Come one, come all!

Cal-Nev-Ha Circle K International presents Fall Training Conference 2014! Come be inspired and entertained by the greatest show on earth!

Fall Training Conference (FTC) is a weekend-long event where members of Cal-Nev-Ha go to Old Oak Ranch in Sonora, CA. In Sonora, you will be able to experience and be exposed to what Circle K is really about and will be able to gain so much knowledge from attending our amazing workshops hosted by members all across the district. In addition, you will be able to see and enjoy creative talent acts and campfire skits.

There will be general sessions filled with hundreds of spirited Circle K members, as well as a special addition of Big Top Brawl, a series of fun, competitive team games where members will get to bond and work together with others from all over CNH. Be a part of our fun, crazy dance and if you aren't feeling the groove, check out alternative activities consisting of a photo booth, movies, and carnival games! Finally, be ready to meet countless new friends and create long lasting Circle K memories!
